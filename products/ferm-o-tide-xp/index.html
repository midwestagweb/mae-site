Fermo-O-Tide XP

Ferm-O-Tide is a proprietary combination of enzymatically hydrolyzed protein sources. It includes porcine intestinal mucosa and vegetable protein. Ferm-O-Tide contains a high ratio of low molecular weight peptides. Ferm-O-Tide is ideally suited to replace or complement spray dried porcine plasma for early weaning pig diets. Ferm-O-Tide can also replace high quality fish meal for early weaned pig, growing-finish pig, lactating sow, broiler, layer, and aquaculture diets. Ferm-O-Tide has also been found to be an excellent attractant for aquaculture.
